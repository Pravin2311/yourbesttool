Understanding Unit Conversion

Unit conversion ensures accuracy and consistency across science, engineering, electronics, cooking, fitness, and daily life. This converter supports:

  • Length: Meters, kilometers, inches, feet, miles.
  • Weight/Mass: Kilograms, grams, pounds, ounces, tons.
  • Volume: Liters, milliliters, gallons, quarts, cups.
  • Temperature: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin.
  • Time: Seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks.
  • Area: Square meters, acres, hectares, square feet.
  • Electronics: Voltage (V), Current (A), Resistance (Ω), Capacitance (F), Inductance (H), Power (W).
  • Physics: Force (N), Energy (J), Power (W), Pressure (Pa), Velocity (m/s), Acceleration (m/s²).
  • Scientific & SI Units: SI prefixes (kilo, milli, micro, nano), Concentration (mol/L), Frequency (Hz), Luminous intensity (cd).
