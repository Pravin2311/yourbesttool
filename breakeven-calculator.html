This advanced Break-Even Calculator helps entrepreneurs and business owners:
- Assess viability of new businesses or product launches.
- Set competitive pricing strategies.
- Evaluate cost structure for expansions.
- Quantify risk and required sales to avoid losses.
- Prepare investor presentations or secure funding.

Key elements:
Fixed Costs: Constant expenses independent of sales volume.
Variable Costs: Costs per unit that vary with production.
Contribution Margin: Selling price per unit minus variable cost per unit.
Break-Even Point: Fixed Costs ÷ Contribution Margin = Units to sell to cover costs.
