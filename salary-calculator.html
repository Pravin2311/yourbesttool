Understand Your Salary Components & Taxation

Gross Salary / CTC: Total cost to company including salary, allowances, bonuses, and benefits.

Basic Salary: Core component used for PF calculation and other benefits.

HRA (House Rent Allowance): Tax-exempt if living in a rented house.

LTA (Leave Travel Allowance): Tax-free travel within India under certain conditions.

Bonus: Performance or annual bonus included in salary.

Other Allowances: Medical, conveyance, or incentive allowances.

Provident Fund (PF): Mandatory retirement contribution, usually 12% of basic.

Professional Tax (PT): State-mandated monthly deduction.

Income Tax: Tax calculated on taxable income (gross - PF - exemptions) based on your tax slab.
